BBF is a three-letter acronym which has multiple meanings:

  • Balance brought forward, a banking term.
  • Belgian Badminton Federation
  • Best bin first, a search algorithm finding an approximate solution to the nearest neighbor search problem
  • Bobby Bowden Field at Doak Campbell Stadium, the football stadium on the campus of the Florida State University in Tallahassee,
  • Boston By Foot, a non-profit organization offering guided architectural and historical tours of Boston, Massachusetts.
  • Boys Before Flowers
  • British Baseball Federation
  • British Best Friend, a series by Paris Hilton, of which the title is a pun on BFF, for Best Friends Forever'.'
  • Broadband Forum, a non-profit industry consortium dedicated to developing broadband network specifications.
