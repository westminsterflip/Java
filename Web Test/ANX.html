Anx is a studio album by American hip hop duo Dark Time Sunshine.[7] It was released on Fake Four Inc. in 2012.[8] The album features guest appearances from the likes of P.O.S, Aesop Rock, and Busdriver.[9]

Reception[edit]

The album was chosen by Impose Magazine as one of the Best Music of July 2012.[10]
