Ntu is a village in the Peren district of Nagaland, India. It is located in the Tening Circle.[1]

Demographics[edit]

According to the 2011 census of India, Ntu has 209 households. The effective literacy rate (i.e. the literacy rate of population excluding children aged 6 and below) is 76.35%.[2]
