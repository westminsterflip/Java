JBZ is a record production team consisting of Marc JB from Bimbo Jones Bimbo Jones and Simon Bartholemew from Brand New Heavies pioneering the sound of iDISCO.[1]

JBZ are currently collaborating on a studio album with disco generation artists including Omar Jocelyn Brown, Kenny Thomas,[2] Aurora Dawn from Alabama 3, Junior Giscombe and Beverley Knight.[3]

JBZ have also performed live at Brixton Jamm [4][5]
