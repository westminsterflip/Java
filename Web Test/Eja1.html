Eja (born Siti Shahrizah bt Saifuddin on 18 January 1972 at Kuala Kubu Baru, Selangor, Malaysia) is a Malaysian actress of television and film. She first gained fame as the co-host in Roda Impian, the Malaysian version of Wheel of Fortune. After leaving the game show, she hosted a number of TV shows and became an actress through a number of drama series.
