Places[edit]

  • BR postcode area, a group of eight postal districts in southeast London
  • Baton Rouge, Louisiana, United States
  • Brazil (BR), according to:
    • Its two-letter country code defined in ISO 3166-1 alpha-2
    • The prefix for Brazil's ISO 3166-2 subdivision codes
    • FIPS 10-4, used by the U.S. Government for geographical data processing in many publications
  • The Province of Brindisi, according to Italy's ISO 3166-2 subdivision codes
  • Brown County, Kansas, United States, according to its Kansas state county code, which is used on the state's license plates
  • Brăila, a town and county in Romania. BR is used in the county's license plates

Science[edit]

  • Bromine (Br), chemical element with atomic number 35
  • the standard abbreviation for Polybutadiene, a type of rubber
  • Bacteriorhodopsin

Computing[edit]

  • .br, country code top-level domain for Brazil
  • Newline element in:
    • XHTML (<br />)
    • HTML (<br>)
  • Bombing Run, a team-based gametype introduced in Unreal Tournament 2003 and Unreal Tournament 2004.
  • Block / Remove, used in social networks and dating sites, as well as instant messaging programs

Music and media[edit]

  • Battle Royale, a Japanese book, movie and manga
  • Bad Religion, an American punk-rock band
  • Bleacher Report, an online sports media network
  • Bohemian Rhapsody
  • Bayerischer Rundfunk (Bavarian Broadcasting), a regional broadcasting service in Germany

Other uses[edit]

  • BR = Best Regards, when it appears at the end of an e-mail, before the sender's name.

  • Bike and ride, bicycle parks with public transport connections
