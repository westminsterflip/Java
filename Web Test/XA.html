XA, xA, or xa may refer to:

As Initials[edit]

  • Chi Alpha, a Christian college group
  • Extended addressing, a processor architecture first introduced by IBM in 1981 in the System/370 model 3081 mainframe computer
  • eXtended Architecture, a standard for Global Transactions. X/Open_XA
  • eXtended Architecture, a standard for CD-ROM. CD-ROM_XA

Technology[edit]

  • Scion xA, a car
  • X/Open XA, a distributed transaction processing specification
  • Fantom-X, a synthesizer made by Roland Corporation
  • Olympus XA, a camera
  • a Maxis proprietary audio format for The Sims and SimCity 3000. Decoding is supported by FFmpeg
  • ".xa". or ".XA", audio file extensions used in PlayStation and PSone

Politics[edit]

  • Golden Dawn (political party), from the Greek initials.
