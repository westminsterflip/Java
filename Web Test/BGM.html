BGM can mean:

  • an abbreviation for the former Bell Globemedia, now Bell Media
  • Biannual General Meeting example: Friends of the Earth (FOEI BGM)
  • The US Military designation for a surface attack guided missile with multiple launch environments. The missile should not need to be substantially modified for different launch environments. Examples include the BGM-71 TOW missile and the BGM-109 Tomahawk.
