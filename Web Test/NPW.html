Neuropeptide W is a protein that in humans is encoded by the NPW gene.[3][4][5]

Neuropeptide W (NPW) is an endogenous peptide ligand for GPR8 (MIM 600731), a G protein-coupled receptor.[supplied by OMIM][5]
