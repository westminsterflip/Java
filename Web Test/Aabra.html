Aabra is an uninhabied village in Rõuge Parish, Võru County, in southeastern Estonia.[1] It's located on the territory of Haanja Nature Park, about 8 km (5 mi) southeast of Rõuge, the administrative centre of the municipality, and about 18 km (11 mi) south of the nearest town Võru.

There's a 1.5 ha (3.7 acres) lake named Lake Aabra and two hills Jaanimägi and Pähkünämägi located on the village.
