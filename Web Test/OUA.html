Oua

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to: navigation, search
This article is about the island in Tuvalu. For the island in Tonga, see 'O'ua. For other uses, see Oua (disambiguation).

Oua is an islet on the north-east of Nukufetau atoll, Tuvalu, next to Lafanga atoll.[1][2]
