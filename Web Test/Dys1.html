Dys [dÉ¨s] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Niemce, within Lublin County, Lublin Voivodeship, in eastern Poland.[1] It lies approximately 7 kilometres (4 mi) south-west of Niemce and 8 km (5 mi) north of the regional capital Lublin.

The village has a population of 1,200.
