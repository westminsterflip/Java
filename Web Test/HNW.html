HNW, Inc. (HNW) is a strategic marketing company based in New York, NY. Founded in 2001, HNW is a provider of strategic and interactive marketing solutions for wealth management firms and luxury brands. HNW is focused exclusively on rich people and offers consulting and creative services.

Core Offerings[edit]

HNW offers services in three key areas:

Strategy, including strategic planning services including marketing plan development, client segmentation strategy, competitive assessment, positioning and value proposition, branding and identity development, new product launches, campaign management and optimization and website analysis. HNW also offers research capabilities, which consist of traditional market research and knowledge management.

Marketing Services, including custom publishing (e.g., online publications and whitepapers); creative services (e.g., logo and mark creation, graphic design and layout services, copywriting and multimedia design); and collateral development (e.g., brochure systems, presentations and seminars.

Interactive Solutions, including web design, building and analytics; web development; information architecture; SEM/SEO; content management; e-commerce and rich Internet application development.

HNW E-Marketing Tools[edit]

HNW also offers a suite of e-marketing technology solutions called HNW eSuite consisting of:

HNW Publisher: An enterprise e-newsletter development and publishing platform, including custom and syndicated content delivery.

HNW Campaign Manager: An e-mail campaign development and management tool and associated spam strategy and creative services.

HNW Microsites: A scalable microsite development solution that integrates site level personalization and with full corporate compliance and control.[citation needed]
