Arts and media[edit]

Music[edit]

  • Main droite/mano destra (abbreviated MD or m.d.), used in piano scores to mark sequences of notes that are to be played with the right hand
  • MiniDisc, an optical storage medium developed by Sony
  • Music director; the term "MD" is used particularly in musical theatre

Other media[edit]

  • MDs (TV Series), a 2002 made-for-television medical drama
  • Materiali e discussioni per l'analisi dei testi classici, a classics journal
  • Mega Drive, a game console produced by Sega

In business[edit]

  • Managing director, a term used for the chief executive of many companies
  • McDonnell Douglas (aircraft mdoel prefix MD), a former aerospace manufacturer that later merged with Boeing
  • Air Madagascar (IATA airline code MD)

Places[edit]

  • Maryland (US postal abbreviation MD)
  • Moldova (ISO country code MD)
    • .md, Internet top-level domain for Moldova
  • Magdeburg, a city in Germany (vehicle plate prefix)
  • Metropolitan Division, a territory unit defined by the US Office of Management and Budget as a county or group of counties within a Metropolitan Statistical Area

Science and technology[edit]

Biology and medicine[edit]

  • Doctor of Medicine, from the Latin Medicinae Doctor (MD)
  • Muscular dystrophy, a genetic and hereditary muscle disease
  • Medial dorsal nucleus, a large nucleus in the thalamus
  • Maladaptive daydreaming, a psychological disorder where excessive daydreaming replaces human interaction

Computing[edit]

  • .md, Internet top-level domain for Moldova
  • "md", alternative form of computer operating system command Mkdir, that creates a directory
  • Markdown format (file extension .md)
  • Message-digest algorithm, a cryptographic hashing function
  • Microdrive, a type of miniature hard drive
  • multiple device, a device driver used to support RAID in the Linux kernel.

Other uses in science and technology[edit]

  • Mean difference, a measure of statistical dispersion
  • Mendelevium, a chemical element with symbol Md
  • Mesoscale Discussion, a short-term technical meteorological forecast concerning convection (MCD) or precipitation (MPD)
  • Methyldichloroarsine, a blister agent used in chemical warfare
  • Millidarcy (mD), one thousandth of a darcy, a unit of fluid permeability
  • Molecular dynamics, a method of computer simulation of molecular interaction

Other uses[edit]

  • Majority decision, a winning criterion in several full-contact combat sports
  • Man-day, amount of work performed by the average worker in one day
  • Major Douglas, a British Engineer and pioneer of the Social Credit economic reform movement
  • Muhammad (name) or Mohammed, as abbreviated in Bangladesh, India, Pakistan and South East Asia
