FDL is an abbreviation for:

  • Federation of Italian Liberals, more commonly known in Italian as Federazione dei Liberali Italiani
  • Fiber Delay Line
  • File Definition Language, in OpenVMS
  • Film Digital Line, Model FDL60 and FDL90 Telecine from Robert Bosch GmbH, Fernseh Div.
  • Firedoglake, a political blog
  • Fleur-de-lis
  • Flexible and Distributed Learning see e-learning
  • Flexor digitorum longus muscle
  • Flow Definition Language
  • Fond du Lac (disambiguation), several places in the United States and Canada
  • Food, Drug, and Liquor, as in retail channels for selling alcohol beverages.
  • Foresight Desktop GNU/Linux
  • Fossa dei Leoni
  • Frame Display Lists, an aspect of the Gecko API
  • GNU Free Documentation License
  • US Airforce Flight Dynamics Laboratory

FDL can also be in reference to:

  • The GE FDL series of diesel engines
