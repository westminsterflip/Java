Nuy (Persian: نوي‎‎, also Romanized as Nūy; also known as Nū'ī)[1] is a village in Margavar Rural District, Silvaneh District, Urmia County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 610, in 104 families.[2]
