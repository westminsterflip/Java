KLB is a rock group/boy band from Brazil. The band consists of three brothers from São Paulo: Kiko (Franco Finato Scornavacca), Leandro Finato Scornavacca and Bruno Finato Scornavacca. KLB stands for each brother's name.

Music career[edit]

The brothers began their career in 1996, with a group named "The Fenders".

KLB started in 1998 for the first time when the brothers performed at Anhembi, São Paulo, in an award, contemplating their father Franco Scornavacca, who was receiving the "Best Brazilian Manager of the 50's). Their first single "A Dor Desse Amor" charted #1 in Brazil.[citation needed]
