Eua is a genus of air-breathing land snails, terrestrial pulmonate gastropod mollusks in the family Partulidae.

Species[edit]

Species within the genus Eua include:

  • Eua expansa
  • Eua globosa
  • Eua montana
  • Eua zebrina
